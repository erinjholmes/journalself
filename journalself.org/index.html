An Open Access Framework For Organizing & Publishing Scholarly Work

We develop software protocols that enable researchers to organize, write, review, and archive scientific papers, presentations, and other digital artifacts.

Sign up for beta

We enable researchers to share work online with tools aimed at maximizing the impact, visibility, and efficiency of research across connecting fields of academia.

Developed For Researchers, By Researchers

JournalSelf is forged from the experience of scientists who help shape protocols to organize, publish, and peer review scholarly work with fewer mediators. We offer a community of professionals who collaborate, review, and share research data using new approaches.

Interoperability That Is Economical And Fast

Publish papers at various stages of research, collect feedback, make improvements, connect with others doing similar work. JournalSelf was built to support open access protocols for various stages of research and publishing by making use of existing databases to easily search and extend a robust catalog of research.

Protocols That Support Academic Processes

Publishing in a Journal can take years after completion of research. Prevent the loss of key research elements by publishing research and other artifacts in stages and milestones.

Move Forward In An Inclusive Environment

JournalSelf is open to all research outputs across converging fields of science, humanities, social science, tech and engineering. We welcome research from all over the world, and from diverse disciplines.
